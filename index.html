About Me

For the past seven years, I have taught music in the DFW metroplex. As a teacher, I have found that the best way to reach a student was to show them WHY they should learn. I did so by being clear in my instructions and detailed in my lesson planning.

In my opinion, Web Development follows the same concept. Effective websites are able to attract users because they are structured well, look great, allowing the clients message display clearly on the screen: their "WHY".

I enjoy creating fully responsive websites that are effective in doing just that. By using a clear design that is simple and easy to follow, I can help clients successfully convey their message to prospective visitors on their phone or computer screen!
